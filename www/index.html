Projects

Inventory management system

The company manage thousands of computers and carry out servicing and repairs on them. Our system provided a simple interface to move items between warehouse locations as well as provide tracking and auditing of item movements. The process was streamlined using a barcode scanner and automatically printed labels for items entering the warehouse.
We built the inventory management system from scratch using PHP, JavaScript and MySQL.

Job Control system

The company expanded into on site repairs and servicing and needed an efficient way to manage jobs, repair parts and technicians. This system allowed a job controller to assign jobs to technicians, add comments and track jobs as they progressed. When allocating parts to the job the inventory management system would be queried to make sure it was in stock.
The technicians accessed the system on mobile devices. They could view their jobs for the day and comment on or update the status of jobs.
